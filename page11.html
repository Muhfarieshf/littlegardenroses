Products FAQ

Why do we don't use fresh flower?

Fresh flowers and preserved flowers are too delicate for this flower arrangements technique. High quality faux silk flower are more lasting.

Can I customise an order?

For custom orders need to be through on Whatsapp. Custom order are not available for now.

Why my order not a replicate of the sample image posted?

Every design is lovingly handcrafted, so you may see a slight difference in the design, just like all flower are not exactly identical.

Can I change my mind and change the design after confirmation?

As you order likely in process of making with new raw materials reserved for you, so you really need to confirm your design before making payments to confirm your order.

Can I refund after confirmation?

You can get a refunds only 2 hours after the payment. After that there will be no refund as these materials have been purchased for you order.

What are your delivery charge?

RM1.00 per kilometer.

Do you do same day delivery?

Yes, it is possible if your order is confirmed before 12:00 p.m and subjected to availability.

Can I self-collect my order?

Yes, you can certainly can and can be collected at Kajang.
