About Us

Our Story: From Passion to Bloom

Welcome to Little Garden Roses!

Founded in 2021, Little Garden Roses began as a small dream to bring the beauty of flowers into the hearts and homes of people everywhere. Our journey started with a passion for creativity and a love for the delicate charm of roses, which led us to discover the enchanting world of balloon flowers.

Objective and Goals

To create a comprehensive and well-planned online presence for Little Garden Roses, showcasing the beauty and variety of flower bouquets, while also providing a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for customers.
